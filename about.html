Welcome all from of the Viking Community! Some of you have heard of Amplified and some have not. Below we explain exactly what we do, and why it impacts you, as well as your school.

Who runs Amplified?

Many are concerned about anonymous comments and punishment for expressing their opinions on this site. We are proud to announce that Amplified is entirely run by students, and no one can be punished for expressing their true but respectful opinions. After all, we are your voice!

What does Amplified do?

Amplified was created to better learning environments by providing a platform where students, and teachers can express their opinions, and concerns regarding their school. You will be able to constructively discuss topics that you think should change.
